About the PetBoot GPS Tracker

The PetBoot GPS Tracker is designed with both pets and owners in mind. Using advanced GPS technology, it provides accurate real-time location data directly to your smartphone. Whether your pet is in the backyard or on an adventure, you'll always know their whereabouts.

Beyond location tracking, the PetBoot monitor's your pet's activity levels, providing insights into their health and wellbeing. The geofencing feature allows you to set safe zones and receive instant alerts if your pet leaves those areas.

With a durable, waterproof design and long-lasting battery, the PetBoot GPS Tracker is the reliable companion your pet needs for their adventures and your peace of mind.
