Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Pet Bot

How does Pet Bot interact with my pet?

Pet Bot uses a combination of sensors, cameras, and AI algorithms to detect your pet's presence and movements. It can initiate games, dispense treats, and respond to voice commands, creating an engaging experience for your pet.

Is Pet Bot safe for my pet?

Absolutely! Pet Bot is designed with pet safety as the top priority. It uses pet-safe materials, has no small parts that could be swallowed, and the laser pointer is Class 1, completely safe for pets. All interactions are monitored to ensure they're positive experiences.

What pets is Pet Bot suitable for?

Pet Bot is ideal for cats and small to medium dogs. The device can be customized in the app based on your pet's size, energy level, and preferences. We don't recommend it for very large dogs or pets with aggressive behavior.

How do I set up and control Pet Bot?

Setting up Pet Bot is simple! Just download the Pet Bot app, connect to your home Wi-Fi, and follow the in-app instructions. You can control all features remotely through the app, set schedules, and receive notifications about your pet's activity.
